Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build my site?

Most sites launch within 10-14 days. Enterprise projects may take 3-4 weeks depending on complexity. We'll give you an exact timeline before you commit.

What if I already have a website?

We'll rebuild it from scratch on a faster, more conversion-focused platform. Your existing content and SEO value are preserved. The transition is seamless for your visitors.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Absolutely. 60% of our Starter clients upgrade to Growth within 6 months. You'll pay the difference in setup fee and the new monthly rate going forward.

What's the monthly fee for?

Hosting, speed optimization, security updates, monthly content changes, and performance monitoring. Think of it as having a web team on retainer — without the full-time cost.

What if I want to cancel?

No long-term contracts. Cancel monthly with 30 days notice. You keep your website — we'll transfer all files and hosting to you. We'd rather earn your business every month than lock you in.
