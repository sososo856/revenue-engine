## Close-call objection handling (for reference — do NOT include in proposal doc)

- **"It's more than I expected"** → "Totally fair. Let's look at what you'd leave behind if we remove the bot and the ad landing pages — here's the Starter at $997. But understand: most of the lead lift in that case study you read came from the bot handling after-hours traffic. Your call."
- **"Can I think about it?"** → "Of course. What specifically do you want to think about? Pricing, timing, or scope?"
- **"I need to talk to my partner/spouse"** → "Makes sense. Can we schedule a 15-min call with both of you Thursday at 3? I'll walk them through the same thing."
- **"Can you do it cheaper?"** → "I can drop scope. I can't drop price without dropping something that drives leads. Which deliverable is the one you'd cut?"
- **"Send me the proposal and I'll review"** → Already doing that. Follow up in 48h with: "Did anything in the proposal surprise you or raise a question?"
