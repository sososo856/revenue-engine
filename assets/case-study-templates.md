## FILLED EXAMPLE: Chiropractor Case Study (Hypothetical but realistic)

### Template 1 Format — Quick Win

### Chiropractic Practice — San Antonio, TX

**The Problem:**
A family chiropractic practice was getting 800 visitors/month to their website but only converting 4 into leads — a 0.5% conversion rate. Their main challenges were:
- Site loaded in 6.8 seconds on mobile (Google recommends under 3)
- No call-to-action visible without scrolling on the homepage
- Contact form had 7 fields including "How did you hear about us?" and "Preferred appointment time"
- Zero Google reviews displayed on the site despite having 47 five-star reviews
- FAQ page was a static wall of text that nobody read

**What We Did:**
- Rebuilt the site from scratch on Next.js + Vercel, cutting load time from 6.8 seconds to 1.4 seconds
- Designed a mobile-first homepage with a prominent "Book Your Adjustment" button above the fold
- Replaced the 7-field form with a 3-field LeadCatch form (Name, Phone, "What brings you in?")
- Pulled 47 Google reviews and displayed them prominently on the homepage with star ratings
- Installed an FAQ chatbot trained on the practice's top 15 questions, connected to their booking system
- Added a video testimonial from a long-time patient (30 seconds, unscripted)

**The Results (in 60 days):**
- Leads per month: 4 → 19 (375% increase)
- Page load time: 6.8s → 1.4s
- Form completion rate: 12% → 34%
- FAQ bot conversations: 90+ per month
- Appointments booked directly via bot: 14 per month
- Estimated additional monthly revenue: $8,000-12,000

**Client Quote:**
"I couldn't believe the difference. We went from maybe one website inquiry per week to getting multiple every day. The chatbot alone books appointments while we're sleeping. I wish we'd done this a year ago."

### Template 2 Format — Deep Dive

### How a San Antonio Chiropractor Went From 4 to 19 Leads Per Month (375% Increase)

**Background:**
Dr. Maria Chen has run a family chiropractic practice in San Antonio for 8 years. The practice has a strong reputation — 47 five-star Google reviews and a loyal patient base built through word of mouth. But online lead generation had always been a weak spot. Despite investing in a website three years ago, the practice was only getting 4-5 new patient inquiries per month from the site.

**The Challenge:**
Dr. Chen's website was a standard WordPress template that looked acceptable on desktop but had significant issues affecting lead generation:

- Mobile performance was poor: 6.8-second load time, which means more than half of mobile visitors left before the page appeared
- The homepage opened with a stock photo slider and a paragraph of mission statement text — no clear path to booking
- The contact form asked for 7 pieces of information, resulting in a 12% completion rate (industry benchmark for healthcare: 30-40%)
- Despite having 47 five-star Google reviews, none were visible on the website
- The FAQ page was a static text page with 23 questions in small font — analytics showed an average time on page of 8 seconds (nobody was reading it)
- After-hours traffic (6pm-8am) represented 40% of total visits, but there was no way for those visitors to engage

**Our Approach:**

*Phase 1: Audit (Days 1-3)*
We ran a comprehensive website audit covering speed, mobile experience, lead capture, trust signals, and conversion clarity. We also analyzed the top 3 competing chiropractors in San Antonio:
- Competitor A had a chat widget and loaded in 2.1 seconds
- Competitor B had online booking and reviews on their homepage
- Competitor C had a blog but slow speed (5.4 seconds) and no lead capture

Dr. Chen's site ranked last among all 4 on every conversion metric. The opportunity was clear: significant room for improvement with proven solutions.

*Phase 2: Build (Days 4-14)*
We rebuilt the site from scratch using Next.js and Tailwind CSS, hosted on Vercel:

**Homepage redesign:** Clean, mobile-first layout. The headline reads "Expert Chiropractic Care in San Antonio — Feel Better Today." Below it: a bright green "Book Your Adjustment" button and a secondary "Call Us: (210) XXX-XXXX" link.

**Social proof integration:** 47 Google reviews displayed in a scrolling carousel on the homepage. Average star rating (4.9) shown prominently with the review count.

**Lead capture overhaul:** Replaced the 7-field form with a 3-field LeadCatch form: Name, Phone Number, "What brings you in?" (dropdown: New patient visit, Existing patient, Back/neck pain, Sports injury, Other). LeadCatch sends an instant SMS notification to Dr. Chen's phone and an automated confirmation email to the patient.

**FAQ chatbot deployment:** Trained an AI chatbot on the practice's top 15 questions:
- Do you accept [insurance name]?
- What should I expect on my first visit?
- How much does an adjustment cost without insurance?
- Do you treat children?
- What are your hours?
- Where are you located / parking?
- Do I need a referral?
- Does chiropractic care hurt?
- How many visits will I need?
- Do you offer massage therapy?
- Can I book same-day appointments?
- What conditions do you treat?
- Do you take walk-ins?
- What should I bring to my first appointment?
- Are X-rays required?

The chatbot answers these instantly and captures name + phone number during the conversation. It also connects to the practice's booking system to schedule appointments directly.

**Video testimonial:** Recorded a 30-second video testimonial with a long-time patient. Unscripted, authentic, filmed on an iPhone in the office. Placed on the homepage below the reviews.

*Phase 3: Launch + Optimize (Days 14-60)*
- Launched the new site and redirected all existing URLs
- Monitored chatbot conversations for the first 2 weeks — added 5 new Q&As based on real questions
- A/B tested the CTA button text ("Book Your Adjustment" vs. "Schedule Your Visit") — "Book Your Adjustment" won by 18%
- Added a seasonal banner in month 2: "New Patient Special: $49 First Visit" — increased form submissions by 22%

**Results:**

| Metric | Before | After | Change |
|--------|--------|-------|--------|
| Monthly leads | 4 | 19 | +375% |
| Page speed (mobile) | 6.8s | 1.4s | -79% |
| Form completion rate | 12% | 34% | +183% |
| Bounce rate | 67% | 38% | -43% |
| Bot conversations/month | 0 | 90+ | New |
| Bot-booked appointments | 0 | 14/month | New |
| Est. monthly revenue increase | — | $8,000-12,000 | — |

**Client Testimonial:**
"When Dan showed me the audit of my old site, I was embarrassed. I had no idea my contact form was broken on iPhones or that my site took 7 seconds to load. The new site is incredible — it looks professional, loads instantly, and the chatbot books appointments while we sleep. We went from maybe one online inquiry per week to multiple per day. I've already referred two other practice owners to Dan."

**Key Takeaway:**
The biggest lever in this project wasn't the design. It was removing friction. A faster site kept more visitors. A simpler form converted more of them. A chatbot captured after-hours traffic that was previously lost. And displaying existing social proof (reviews they already had) built trust instantly. Most local businesses don't need more traffic — they need to stop losing the traffic they already have.
