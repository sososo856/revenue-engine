# Client Spotlight Template — Monthly Feature
# Free marketing for the client, drives referrals for you
# Updated: April 2026

---

## Overview

Every month, feature one client in a "Client Spotlight." This serves three purposes:
1. Free marketing for the client (they share it with their audience)
2. Social proof for your agency (shows real results)
3. Referral generation (the client feels appreciated and introduces you to others)

---

## The Client Spotlight Format

### [Month] Client Spotlight: [Business Name]

**The Business:**
[Business Name] is a [type of business] in [City, State], owned by [Owner First Name]. They specialize in [what they do] and have been serving [City] for [X] years. With [X] Google reviews and a [X]-star rating, they've built a reputation for [what they're known for].

**Why We Love Working With [First Name]:**
[2-3 sentences about what makes this client great to work with. Be genuine and specific.]

Example: "Dr. Martinez is one of those clients who trusts the process but also asks great questions. She pushed us to make the FAQ chatbot more detailed — and she was right. The extra answers we added account for 30% of the bot's conversations. Her attention to her patients shows in everything she does."

**What We Built Together:**
- [Deliverable 1 — e.g., "Custom 7-page website with mobile-first design"]
- [Deliverable 2 — e.g., "LeadCatch smart form with instant SMS notifications"]
- [Deliverable 3 — e.g., "FAQ chatbot trained on 15 patient questions"]
- [Deliverable 4 — e.g., "Google review integration on homepage"]

**The Results:**
- [Metric 1 — e.g., "Website leads: 4/month → 19/month"]
- [Metric 2 — e.g., "Page load time: 6.8s → 1.4s"]
- [Metric 3 — e.g., "FAQ bot conversations: 90+/month"]
- [Metric 4 — e.g., "Estimated revenue impact: $8,000-12,000/month additional"]

**In [First Name]'s Words:**
"[Client testimonial — 2-3 sentences, as authentic as possible. Ask them for a quote or use something they've said in conversation.]"

**Check Them Out:**
- Website: [URL]
- Google Maps: [Link]
- Social: [Instagram/Facebook link]

If you're in [City] and need [their service], we highly recommend [Business Name]. Tell them Dan sent you.

## Distribution Checklist

When you publish a Client Spotlight, share it across all channels:

- [ ] Post on LinkedIn (tag the client's business page and owner's personal page)
- [ ] Post on X/Twitter (tag them if they're on the platform)
- [ ] Share on Instagram stories (tag them)
- [ ] Email it to the client: "Hey [First Name], I featured [Business Name] in our monthly client spotlight! Here's the post — feel free to share it with your audience."
- [ ] Email it to your full client list: "Check out this month's client spotlight — [Business Name]"
- [ ] Add it to your website's case studies or portfolio page

## Client Selection Criteria

Pick clients who:
1. Have strong results to showcase (numbers matter)
2. Are active on social media (more likely to share)
3. Are in a niche you want more clients from (the spotlight attracts similar businesses)
4. Have been great to work with (the spotlight should be genuine)
5. Haven't referred anyone recently (the spotlight + email combo often triggers a referral)
