# Results Guarantee — Terms & Messaging
# 30-day money-back if no qualified lead
# Updated: April 2026

---

## The Guarantee

**Statement:** If your new website doesn't generate at least one qualified lead within 30 days of launch, I'll refund your setup fee in full.

---

## Terms & Conditions

### What counts as a "qualified lead":
- A form submission with valid contact information (real name, working phone number or email)
- A chatbot conversation where the visitor provides their contact information and expresses interest in a service
- A phone call or text initiated through the website's click-to-call feature (tracked via call tracking or manually confirmed by the client)

### What does NOT count:
- Spam submissions
- Submissions from the client or their employees
- Bot traffic or automated form fills
- Leads from sources other than the website (walk-ins, referrals, ads running independently)

### Conditions for the guarantee to apply:
1. The client's website must remain live and unmodified for the full 30-day period (no taking it down, no removing the lead capture form, no blocking the chatbot)
2. The client must have some baseline traffic — either organic search traffic, social media presence, or active advertising driving visitors to the site. The guarantee covers website conversion, not traffic generation.
3. The 30-day period starts on the day the site goes live, confirmed in writing via email
4. The client must notify Dan within 7 days after the 30-day period ends to request a refund
5. Refund applies to the one-time setup fee only, not to monthly recurring charges already billed

### Refund process:
1. Client emails Dan stating that no qualified leads were generated
2. Dan reviews analytics and lead capture data to confirm
3. If confirmed, full setup fee refund is processed via Stripe within 5 business days
4. The website remains the client's property — they keep the site even after the refund
5. Monthly service is cancelled unless the client wants to continue

---

## Why This Guarantee Works (Internal Notes)

**Risk is low because:**
- A well-built site with proper lead capture on a business with any traffic will generate leads
- In 18 months, the guarantee has been triggered exactly once (ultra-niche business with near-zero search volume)
- The guarantee screening process (Condition #2 — baseline traffic required) filters out impossible cases

**Business impact:**
- Closes 20-30% more deals because it eliminates buyer risk
- Creates urgency (prospects who are "thinking about it" pull the trigger faster)
- Builds trust and confidence in the offer
- The one refund we gave generated a referral worth more than the original deal

**When NOT to offer it:**
- Brand new businesses with no online presence (no traffic = no leads regardless of site quality)
- Businesses in extremely niche markets with very low search volume
- Clients who want a brochure site with no lead capture
- Projects where the client controls their own ads and might turn them off
