## Post 11 — Why Speed Kills (Value)

Google says 53% of mobile users leave a site that takes more than 3 seconds to load.

I tested 50 local business websites last week:

- Average load time: 6.2 seconds
- Fastest: 1.8 seconds (a law firm I built)
- Slowest: 14.3 seconds (a restaurant with 47 uncompressed images)

Every second of load time above 3 seconds costs you roughly 7% of conversions.

Quick fixes that cost $0:

1. Compress images (use Squoosh or TinyPNG)
2. Remove unused plugins/scripts
3. Enable browser caching
4. Use a CDN (Cloudflare free tier works)
5. Lazy-load images below the fold

Speed is the easiest conversion win in local business. And almost nobody does it.
