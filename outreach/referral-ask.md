# Referral Ask — Message to Every Won Client
# $100 off their next month per introduction
# Updated: April 2026

---

## The Offer

For every introduction a current client makes that leads to a call (not even a close — just a booked call), they get $100 off their next monthly invoice. No limit.

---

## Template 1: Post-Launch Referral Ask (Send within 48 hours of site launch)

**Subject:** Your site is live + a quick favor

Hi [First Name],

Your new website is officially live! Really proud of how it turned out — [specific compliment about their project, e.g., "the before/after gallery looks incredible" or "the booking flow is super smooth"].

Quick favor: if you know any other business owners who could use a website that actually generates leads, I'd love an introduction. It can be as simple as a text or email connecting us.

As a thank-you, I'll take $100 off your next monthly invoice for each introduction — no limit.

Here's a quick blurb you can copy/paste if it helps:

---

*"Hey [Name], I just got a new website built by Dan and it's already generating leads. He specializes in local businesses — builds fast, great to work with, and offers a 30-day results guarantee. Want me to connect you? His site: [your URL]"*

---

Thanks again for trusting me with [Business Name]'s site. Excited to see the leads roll in.

— Dan

Also — I'm looking to bring on a couple more clients this quarter. If anyone in your network could benefit from a website that generates leads (dentist, contractor, gym owner, anyone with a local business), I'd love an introduction.

$100 off your next invoice for each one. And I promise I'll treat them the same way I've treated you.

Let me know either way — always good to hear how things are going.

— Dan

## Making the Referral Easy

Always reduce friction for the referrer. Give them:

1. **A copy/paste message** they can text or email (see Template 1)
2. **Your booking link** they can share: [Calendar link]
3. **Permission to share their results** if they're comfortable: "I can mention your case study to them so they see real results — would that be okay?"

## Tracking Referrals

In your CRM (Notion), track:

|----------|------------------|------|-------------|---------|----------------|
| | | | | | |

Apply the $100 credit on the next invoice after the referred person books a call (regardless of whether they become a client). This encourages future referrals even when the referred lead doesn't close.

---

## Rules

1. Ask for referrals at every natural touchpoint (launch, reports, milestones, check-ins)
2. Never make the ask feel like a requirement — always frame it as a favor
3. Apply the credit promptly and mention it: "Applied your $100 referral credit — thanks again for connecting me with [Name]"
4. Send a thank-you message even if the referral doesn't convert: "Thanks for the intro to [Name]. Even though the timing wasn't right for them, I really appreciate you thinking of me."
5. Track referral sources to identify your best referrers — those clients get extra attention
