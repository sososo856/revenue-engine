# Warm Reactivation — Re-Engage Past "Not Now" and "Too Expensive" Leads
# 10 angles for leads who said no 60-90+ days ago
# Updated: April 2026

---

## When to Use This

- Lead said "not the right time" 60+ days ago
- Lead said "too expensive" 60+ days ago
- Lead went cold after initial interest
- Lead chose a competitor but may not be satisfied

Wait at least 60 days from their last interaction. These are not follow-ups — they're fresh conversations with new angles.

## Execution Plan

1. **Segment your cold leads** by reason they didn't buy:
   - "Not now" → Start with Angle 1 (New Results) or Angle 4 (Seasonal)
   - "Too expensive" → Start with Angle 2 (New Pricing) or Angle 8 (Risk Elimination)
   - "Went with competitor" → Start with Angle 6 (Re-Audit) or Angle 3 (Competitor Intel)
   - "Went cold, no reason given" → Start with Angle 7 (Social Proof) or Angle 5 (New Feature)

2. **Send one reactivation email per lead.** Wait 14 days. If no reply, try one more angle from a different category. After 2 attempts with no reply, archive for 6 months.

3. **Track reactivation results separately** from cold outreach. Reactivation should convert at 5-10% (vs. 2-3% for cold).

4. **Timing matters:** Send Tuesday-Thursday, 9-11am local time. Avoid Mondays (inbox overload) and Fridays (weekend mode).
