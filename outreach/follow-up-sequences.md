**Cut your contact form to 3 fields.** Name, phone number, and "What do you need?" Every extra field beyond 3 drops completion rates by 10-15%. If your form has 6+ fields, you're losing more than half of the people who start filling it out.

## RULES

1. Never send more than one follow-up per week to the same person
2. Every follow-up must add new value — never send "just checking in" without something attached
3. If they ask to stop, stop immediately and mark as "Opted Out" in your CRM
4. After Sequence D or J with no reply, move to "Cold" status and revisit in 60-90 days with the warm reactivation sequence
5. Always reply to the original email thread (same subject line with "Re:") so they see the history
