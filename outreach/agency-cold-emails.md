## General Tips for All Templates

1. **Always fill in the specifics.** Run their site through PageSpeed Insights. Look at their actual form. Count the actual fields. Use real numbers.

2. **Record the Loom before sending.** The video is what makes this work. Without it, you're just another cold email.

3. **Never send more than 10 per day** from a single email address. Keep deliverability high.

4. **Track opens and replies.** Use a tool like Brevo or Mailtrack to see who opens but doesn't reply — those are warm for follow-up.

5. **Match the channel to the business.** Restaurants and salons → Instagram DM. Law firms and accountants → email. HVAC and plumbers → email or LinkedIn.
