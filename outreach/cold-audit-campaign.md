# Cold Audit Campaign — DM 50 Businesses with Free 5-Point Website Audit
# Complete script + audit template + tracking sheet
# Updated: April 2026

---

## Campaign Overview

**Goal:** DM 50 local businesses offering a free 5-point website audit. Convert 15-20% to calls, close 40% of calls.
**Expected results:** 50 DMs → 10 replies → 7 calls → 3 clients ($3,000-6,000 revenue)
**Timeline:** 2 weeks (5 DMs per day, Monday-Friday)
**Channels:** LinkedIn DM, Instagram DM, Email (in order of preference)

---

## Step 1: Find Prospects

**Method:** Google Maps search for local service businesses.

Search queries to use:
- "[service] near [your city]"
- "best [service] in [your city]"
- "[service] [your city] reviews"

**Services to target (highest conversion niches):**
Dentist, HVAC, plumber, chiropractor, law firm, gym/fitness, auto repair, roofing, landscaping, cleaning service

**Qualifying criteria:**
- Has a website (not just a Google Business Profile)
- Website is slow, outdated, or has visible issues
- Business has at least 10 Google reviews (indicates real revenue)
- Not a chain/franchise (they have corporate websites)

**Tool:** Open each website. Run it through pagespeed.web.dev on your phone. Note 3 issues in 60 seconds.

---

## Step 2: Record the Loom Audit

For each qualified prospect, record a 60-90 second Loom video.

**Loom script:**

"Hey [First Name], Dan here. I run a web agency that helps local businesses get more leads from their websites. I was checking out [Business Name]'s site and wanted to share a few things I noticed.

[Share screen showing their website]

First, let's look at speed. [Pull up PageSpeed Insights] Your site scores a [X] on mobile — Google recommends above 90 for optimal performance. This means visitors on phones are waiting [X] seconds, and more than half will leave before the page even loads.

Second, [show the homepage on mobile] on a phone, your call-to-action isn't visible without scrolling. Most visitors make a decision in the first 3 seconds, so that 'Book Now' or 'Call Us' button needs to be right at the top.

Third, [show the contact form] your form asks for [X] fields. In our experience, every field beyond 3 drops completion by 10-15%. Name, phone, and what they need — that's the sweet spot.

These are quick wins that could significantly increase how many people contact you from your site. No pitch here — just thought you'd want to know. If you have any questions, happy to chat."

## Campaign Rules

1. Send exactly 5 per day (Mon-Fri). Don't batch-blast — spread them out.
2. Always personalize. Never send a template without filling in specifics.
3. Record a unique Loom for every prospect. No reusing videos.
4. Follow up 3 times maximum. After that, move on.
5. Track everything. Review pipeline every Sunday.
6. Once a prospect replies, switch to personal conversation. No more templates.
