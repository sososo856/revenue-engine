# Upsell Sequences

The cheapest revenue in your business: clients you already have. Target: **25% of Starter/Growth clients upsell within 90 days.** That's +$3–5K MRR without a single new cold email.

Three sequences, triggered by time + behavior:

1. **Starter → Growth** (fires on day 30 of active Starter client)
2. **Add FAQ Bot** (fires on day 30 of any client without bot — see also `/assets/faq-upsell-pitch.md`)
3. **Growth → Scale** (fires when client shares they're running paid ads, OR at day 60 if lead volume is plateauing)

## Sequence 3 — Growth → Scale

**Who gets it:** Growth clients who (a) mention paid ads OR (b) plateau at day 60+.
**Why it works:** They've saturated what Growth can do. Scale adds ad landing pages + A/B testing + priority support — the stuff that unlocks paid-traffic ROI.
**Expected conversion:** 10–15%, but each close is +$250/mo MRR + $4,997 setup.

## Upsell triggers to automate (even if just reminders in a calendar)

| Trigger | Sequence to send |
|---|---|
| Client hits Day 30 active | Sequence 1 (if Starter) + Sequence 2 (if no bot) |
| Client hits Day 60 active | Sequence 3 (Growth → Scale) if leads <10/mo |
| Client mentions paid ads in any message | Sequence 3 Trigger 1 |
| Client submits a revision for a 3rd service page | Flag: ready for Growth upgrade — send Sequence 1 |
| Client adds a 2nd location | Flag: ready for Scale — send Sequence 3 |
| Client refers someone | Thank-you + discount on next upsell (10% off first month) |

## Rules

1. **Never upsell in week 1.** Let them see results first.
2. **Never upsell two things at once.** Pick the highest-leverage one.
3. **If they say "not now" — back off 30 days, then try a different sequence.** Don't nag the same angle.
4. **Track every upsell email in the pipeline.** Upsell is a stage, not a vibe.
5. **When a client upsells, that is the moment to ask for a referral.** They just doubled down on you — social proof is maximum.
