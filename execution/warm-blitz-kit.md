# 24-Hour Warm Blitz — Turn-Key Kit

This is the executable version of `/pricing/founder-calls.md`. Everything pre-filled so you spend time sending, not writing.

**Expected outcome:** 30 DMs → 15–18 replies → 8–10 calls → 2–3 closes = **$6K–$15K cash in 7 days.**

---

## Hour 0–2: Build the list of 30

Open your phone contacts, email, LinkedIn, Instagram DMs. Fill these 30 slots.

### Rules

- **No perfectionism.** If they own a business and you've spoken in the last 18 months, they go on the list.
- **Don't overthink category assignment** — just pick the closest fit.
- **Skip nobody out of awkwardness.** The uncomfortable DMs convert highest because nobody else sends them.

## Hour 2–5: Send all 30 DMs

Copy the right script for the category. Change the 3 bracketed placeholders per message. Do NOT mass-paste without customizing — people can smell it.

### Script A — Past clients

> Hey {name}! It's been a while — hope {business} is cruising.
>
> I'm blocking off a few 15-min strategy calls this week to help business owners squeeze more leads out of their sites heading into summer. Thought of you first since we've worked together.
>
> Even if everything's running smooth, I'd love to take a quick look and flag anything I see. Want a slot? Got {day} at {time} or {day2} at {time2}.

### Script B — Warm leads who never closed

> Hey {name}, circling back. We chatted about {business}'s site a while back and I wanted to reach out again.
>
> I'm doing something this week: free 15-min calls where I pull up your site, give you 3 specific things to fix for more leads. No follow-up pitch, just the audit.
>
> I've learned a lot since we last talked and I think I could give you some real value. Grab a slot? {day} at {time} or {day2} at {time2}.

### Script C — LinkedIn connections

> Hey {name}, been seeing your posts on {topic they post about} — good stuff.
>
> I'm opening a handful of 15-min strategy calls this week. Free, no strings. I'll pull up {business}'s site and give you 3 actionable things to increase leads.
>
> I do this for a living and genuinely like connecting with more local business owners. Interested? {day} at {time} or {day2} at {time2}.

### Script D — Friends/family

> Hey {name}! Random question — would {business} benefit from more customers finding you online?
>
> I'm running quick 15-min audits this week where I look at someone's website and give them specific fixes. Free — just want to help people I know.
>
> Want me to grab you a slot?

### Script E — Content engagers

> Hey {name}, noticed you've been engaging with my posts — appreciate that.
>
> Wanted to give something back: doing a handful of free 15-min strategy calls this week. I'll pull up {business}'s site, flag 3 things to improve, hand you a mini action plan.
>
> No pitch at the end — just value. Want in?

## Hour 5–24: Reply within 30 minutes

- **Phone notifications ON.** Your reply speed is the single biggest booking variable.
- When they reply yes → send your **Calendly link immediately.** Don't ask "what time works?" — give them the calendar and let them self-book.
- When they ask "what's the catch?" → "No catch. I'll pitch only if I see something I can actually help with. Either way you leave with 3 fixes."
- When they say "can you just send the fixes" → "I could, but half the value is the back-and-forth — your context changes the recommendations. 15 min, promise."

---

## Day 2–3: Run the calls

Use the exact 15-min structure from `/pricing/founder-calls.md` lines 80–100. Recap:

- 0–2 min: "How's business going?"
- 2–5 min: "Tell me about your site — traffic, leads, what's working."
- 5–10 min: Screen-share, walk through 3 issues (speed, form, mobile).
- 10–13 min: Prioritized recommendations.
- 13–15 min: Natural close — "I can build this out. Want a proposal in your inbox tomorrow?"

**Immediately after each call** (within 2 hours):
- If interested → send proposal from `/assets/proposal-template.md`. Subject: "Recap + proposal for {business}"
- If lukewarm → send the 3-fix summary email (Template in founder-calls.md lines 131–147). Follow up in 30 days.

---

## Day 4–7: Close

- Proposal out 48h without reply → "Hey {name}, did anything in the proposal raise a question?"
- Proposal out 5 days without reply → "Still interested or should I take this off the table?" (urgency works)
- Closed → immediately move to onboarding via `/assets/onboarding-checklist.md`. **Kickoff call within 48 hours of signed contract.**
