## The only pipeline metric

**50 cold audits sent per week.** Not 49. That feeds everything.

Conversion assumptions (from your own files):
- 50 audits sent → 10 replies (20%)
- 10 replies → 5 booked calls (50%)
- 5 calls → 1 close at Growth or Scale (20%)
- = **1 new client per week = 4/month at $2,497–$4,997**

If replies drop below 15%, your audit script is stale — rewrite.
If calls drop below 40%, your follow-up sequence broke — fix.
If close rate drops below 15%, your proposal or pricing frame is off — fix the call, not the price.

## What kills this plan

1. **Skipping a day of audit sends.** 50/week or nothing.
2. **Tinkering with the offer.** Growth package is proven. Don't rewrite it — sell it.
3. **Adding channels before cold works.** Partnerships, ads, content = month 2+.
4. **Delivering slow.** A bottleneck at fulfillment kills referrals and cash. Follow the onboarding checklist.
5. **Not raising prices in M3.** You will leave $15–25K/year on the table per client.
