# Pipeline Tracker

Build this as a Notion database or Google Sheet. Spend 30 minutes setting it up. Never skip an update.

---

## The 7 stages

Every lead is in exactly one. Move them the moment the stage changes — not "at end of day."

| # | Stage | Definition | Exit trigger | SLA |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Prospect** | Company researched, Loom not yet recorded | Audit sent | Same day |
| 2 | **Contacted** | Audit sent via email/DM | Reply received OR 7 days with no reply | 7-day F/U cadence |
| 3 | **Engaged** | They replied, interested or asking questions | Call booked OR 2 weeks no call | 48h response |
| 4 | **Call Booked** | Discovery call scheduled | Call happens | — |
| 5 | **Proposal Sent** | Proposal delivered post-call | Signed OR declined OR ghosted 14 days | Proposal out in 2h |
| 6 | **Won** | Contract signed, deposit received | Onboarding complete → move to Clients | — |
| 7 | **Lost / Nurture** | No/ghost/wrong fit — tag reason | Re-engage in 30/60/90 days | 30-day recycle |

## Fields every lead needs

```
- Name (contact)
- Company
- Niche (dentist, HVAC, law firm, etc.)
- Website
- Source (cold audit, referral, warm reactivation, inbound, partnership)
- Stage (from table above)
- Entered stage on (date)
- Next action (text)
- Next action date
- Loom link
- Notes (from call)
- Expected package (Starter / Growth / Scale / DFY)
- Expected deal value (setup + first-year MRR)
- Lost reason (if Lost)
```

## Daily pipeline hygiene (5 minutes before logging off)

1. Any lead with no "Next action date"? → set one or move to Lost.
2. Any lead whose date is in the past? → do the action or reschedule it.
3. Anyone in Contacted for 7+ days with no reply? → send follow-up #2 or move to Lost.
4. Anyone in Proposal for 7+ days? → send the nudge.

If more than 5 leads have stale dates, you are losing deals you already paid for.

## Monthly review (last Friday of month)

Answer in order, write it down:

1. Did I hit $__ target this month? Yes/No. By how much?
2. What channel produced the most revenue per hour spent? (cold outreach / referral / partnership / warm / inbound)
3. What channel produced the least? → drop or rebuild it next month.
4. Which niche closed the fastest and highest? → 60% of next month's audits go there.
5. Which client is most likely to churn? → schedule a save call this week.
6. Who are the next 3 upsell candidates? → queue for first week of next month.
7. What one operational leak cost me money? (slow onboarding, lost proposals, missed follow-ups, delivery bottleneck) → fix before day 5 of next month.

## Stage conversion benchmarks (red-flag if below)

| Conversion | Healthy | Red flag | Fix |
|---|---|---|---|
| Audit → Reply | 20% | <15% | Rewrite hook — first 2 sentences + subject line |
| Reply → Call booked | 50% | <35% | Your reply handler is pitching too soon. Ask 1 discovery question first. |
| Call → Proposal | 90% | <70% | You're not pre-qualifying. Send a Typeform before the call. |
| Proposal → Close | 30% | <20% | Weak proposal OR weak call. Record next 3 calls, review. |
| Close → Onboarded < 5 days | 95% | <80% | Onboarding checklist not being followed. |
| Client → Upsell at day 60 | 25% | <10% | Not running the Day 30 and Day 60 upsell emails. |
