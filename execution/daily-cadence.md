# Daily Cadence

The exact block schedule. Follow it until it's automatic. Violating one block = missing the week's number.

---

## The non-negotiables

- **10 cold audits/day, Mon–Fri** = 50/week. This is the only metric that matters.
- **All DMs and emails answered within 4 hours** during work hours.
- **Every discovery call follow-up sent within 2 hours** of ending the call.
- **Pipeline updated before logging off.** No exceptions.

---

## Monday — Prospecting Day

| Time | Block | Action |
|---|---|---|
| 08:00–08:30 | Review | Open `/execution/90-day-plan.md`. Check last week's numbers vs target. Write today's 3 priorities. |
| 08:30–10:30 | **Audit production** | Record 10 Loom audits. Use 1 niche from `/outreach/agency-cold-emails.md`. Batch: 3 sites → write 3 emails → record 3 Looms → send. Repeat. |
| 10:30–11:30 | Follow-ups | Hit every lead sitting in "Contacted" for 3+ days with `/outreach/follow-up-sequences.md`. |
| 11:30–12:30 | Calls | Take whatever is booked. |
| 13:30–15:30 | Delivery | Client work. Ship something — a page, a bot setup, a revision. |
| 15:30–16:30 | Content | Post 1 LinkedIn post from `/content/linkedin-posts.md`. Engage 20 minutes with ICP profiles. |
| 16:30–17:00 | Wrap | Update pipeline. Log numbers in `/execution/pipeline-tracker.md` weekly dashboard. |

## Friday — Review & Load Day

| Time | Block | Action |
|---|---|---|
| 08:00–08:30 | Review | This week's numbers. Did you send 50 audits? Be honest. |

| 11:00–12:30 | Calls | Booked calls. |

| 16:00–17:00 | Wrap + off | Hard stop. Do nothing work-related until Monday unless a proposal is going cold. |

## Emergency rules

- **If you miss a day of audits** → add 3 to each remaining day that week. Do not roll to next week.
- **If replies drop below 15% for 2 weeks straight** → rewrite your audit opening hook. Test 3 variants.
- **If close rate drops below 15% for 2 weeks** → the problem is the call, not the price. Record a call, review with a mentor/peer.
- **If you're too busy to send audits** → you are exactly the wrong amount of busy. Delivery can slip a day; pipeline cannot.
