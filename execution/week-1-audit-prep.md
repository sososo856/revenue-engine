# Week 1 — Loom Audit Prep Kit

Ten audit slots, pre-scripted. All you do is pick a business in the niche, fill the 3 blanks, hit record. Each Loom takes ~8 minutes end-to-end (2 min research, 90 sec record, 30 sec upload, 3 min email). Ten Looms = ~80 min of focused work.

---

## Before you start (one-time, 5 min)

- [ ] Install the free Loom desktop app + Chrome extension ([loom.com](https://loom.com))
- [ ] Set up a Gmail template called "Loom Audit" with your email body (see below)
- [ ] Open 5 browser tabs: the prospect's site, Google PageSpeed Insights, mobile view (Chrome DevTools), their Google Business Profile, their top competitor
- [ ] Rename your Loom to: `{BusinessName} - 60 sec site audit`
- [ ] Loom setting: **Cam-bubble bottom-left, screen on right** (you want them to see your face briefly, then focus on the site)

---

## The universal audit script (90 seconds, say exactly this)

```
Hey {first name}, quick 60-second look at {their domain} — I do this for {niche}
businesses and wanted to share 3 things I'd fix to get you more leads this month.

[SCROLL HOME PAGE]
Number one — {specific issue, e.g., "your hero doesn't say what city you serve or
what you do until I scroll two screens"}. A visitor makes a decision in 5 seconds.
You're asking them to work.

[SHOW PAGESPEED SCORE]
Number two — your site takes {X seconds} to load on mobile. Industry benchmark is
under 2. Every extra second costs you about 7% of leads, per Google's own data.

[SCROLL TO CONTACT FORM OR BOT]
Number three — {missing element, e.g., "no chatbot or quick-response form. When
someone lands here at 8pm, they have to wait till tomorrow to hear back. Most will
be on a competitor's site by then"}.

Happy to send the written breakdown if useful — reply to this email and I'll get
it over today.
```

**Stop talking at 90 seconds.** Do not pitch. Do not name a price. Do not apologize. Just end.

## Fastest way to find 10 prospects (10 minutes)

1. Google search: `{niche} near {city you're targeting}` → Maps results
2. Open the top 5 listings per niche → check their website
3. Skip any site that's already on Squarespace, Webflow, Shopify looking clean. You want **outdated, slow, or missing mobile optimization** — those are audit gold.
4. Grab owner name from their About page or their Google Business Profile
5. Find email via Hunter.io (free: 25/month), Apollo (free: 50/month), or just `info@{domain}`. If nothing — LinkedIn DM the owner instead.

## Fastest way to find 3 issues per site (2 min each)

Do these in order, every single time:

1. **Mobile view** (Chrome DevTools, iPhone 12 preset) → Is the phone number tappable? Is there a CTA above the fold? Is text readable without pinching?
2. **PageSpeed Insights** → Is mobile score under 60? Is LCP over 4 seconds? Screen-grab the bad number.
3. **Contact page** → Is the form 5+ fields? Is there a chatbot? Is there a phone number? Is there after-hours expectation-setting?

Those 3 checks alone give you your 3 issues. Every site. Every niche.


**If you hit 2 replies and 1 call in Week 1**, you're on pace. Scale to 50/week starting Week 2.
