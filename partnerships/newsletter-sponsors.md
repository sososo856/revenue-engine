## Selection Criteria

**Must-haves:**
- Audience includes local business owners, agency owners, or marketing managers
- Subscriber count: 1,000-20,000 (sweet spot for sub-$500 pricing)
- Open rate above 30% (ask for their media kit)
- At least weekly send frequency
- Sponsor pricing under $500 per placement

**Nice-to-haves:**
- Niche-focused (dental marketing, HVAC business, contractor newsletters)
- Engaged audience (high click rates, replies, community)
- Newsletter author has credibility in the space
- Previous sponsors are relevant (not random consumer products)

**Red flags:**
- No media kit or audience stats available
- Audience is mostly other agencies (they're competitors, not buyers)
- Newsletter is mostly promotional content (low trust from readers)
- No examples of past sponsor integrations

## Pitch Template

**Subject:** Sponsoring [Newsletter Name] — quick question

Hi [Newsletter Author Name],

I'm Dan — I run a web agency and a SaaS product (LeadCatch) focused on helping local businesses generate more leads from their websites.

I've been reading [Newsletter Name] for a while and think your audience would find our offer genuinely useful. I'd love to sponsor an upcoming issue.

**What I'd want to promote:**
[Choose one per sponsorship:]
- Option A: Free website audit offer (great for lead gen — no hard sell to their audience)
- Option B: LeadCatch product with a special discount for subscribers
- Option C: $27 Website Conversion Checklist (digital product, low friction)

**What I'd provide:**
- Sponsor copy (you can edit to match your voice)
- A dedicated landing page for tracking
- A genuine offer that helps your readers (not a bait-and-switch)

**Questions:**
1. What's your current sponsorship rate?
2. What does a typical sponsor integration look like in your newsletter?
3. Do you have a media kit with audience demographics and engagement stats?

Happy to work with your format and voice. I want this to feel like a recommendation, not an ad.

Thanks,
Dan

## Tracking & ROI

For every newsletter sponsorship, create a unique:
- Landing page URL (e.g., yoursite.com/[newsletter-name])
- Discount code (if applicable)
- UTM parameters for analytics

Track:

| Newsletter | Date | Cost | Impressions (est.) | Clicks | Leads | Customers | Revenue | ROI |
|-----------|------|------|--------------------|--------|-------|-----------|---------|-----|
| | | | | | | | | |

**Target ROI:** 3-5x on spend. A $300 sponsorship should generate at least $900-1,500 in pipeline.

**If ROI is positive:** Book recurring monthly or bi-weekly sponsorship.
**If ROI is negative:** Test different copy or offer before abandoning the newsletter.
