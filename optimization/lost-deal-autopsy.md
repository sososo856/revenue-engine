## Monthly Pattern Review

At the end of each month, review all lost deals and look for patterns:

**Questions to answer:**
1. What's the #1 reason I'm losing deals? (If it's price → adjust packaging. If it's competition → sharpen positioning. If it's timing → improve follow-up.)
2. Which lead source produces the most losses? (Some channels attract lower-quality leads.)
3. At which stage am I losing the most deals? (Proposal stage → proposal needs work. Call stage → call framework needs work.)
4. Am I consistently losing to the same competitor? (If yes, build a specific comparison for sales calls.)
5. How many lost deals did I retry, and what was the retry conversion rate?

**Goal:** Reduce lost deal rate by 5% per quarter by fixing the primary pattern.
