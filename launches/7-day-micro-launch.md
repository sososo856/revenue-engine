## What You're Launching

This framework works for any offer. Adapt it for:
- New product (LeadCatch feature, chatbot offering)
- New package (bundle, premium tier)
- Limited-time offer (seasonal discount, founding member pricing)
- Digital product (swipe file, templates)

**Example launch used throughout:** "Local Business Growth Kit" bundle at founding member pricing ($797 setup + $297/mo instead of $997 + $397/mo). Limited to first 10 buyers.

> **Pricing derivation:** Individual monthly totals: $127/mo (site) + $197/mo (LeadCatch Pro) + $297/mo (FAQ bot) = $621/mo. Regular bundle: $397/mo (36% bundle discount). Founding member: $297/mo.

## Stripe Setup

1. Create a product in Stripe: "Local Business Growth Kit — Founding Member"
2. Price: $797 (one-time) + $297/month (subscription)
3. Create a Payment Link with both charges
4. Set the payment link to auto-deactivate after 10 purchases (or manually close it)
5. Connect Stripe webhook to Make.com → Send welcome email via Brevo → Add to Notion CRM
