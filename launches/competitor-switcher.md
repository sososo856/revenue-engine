## How to Find Switcher Prospects

### Method 1: Technology detection
Visit local business websites. Check for known chat widgets or form builders:
- Look for "Powered by Typeform" or "Powered by Tidio" badges
- Check page source for script tags (typeform.com, drift.com, intercom.io)
- Use BuiltWith or Wappalyzer browser extension

### Method 2: Search
- Google: "[competitor name] review local business" — find people complaining
- Reddit: "Typeform alternative" or "[tool] not working" threads
- Facebook groups for local business owners — listen for tool complaints

### Method 3: From your own pipeline
- Past leads who said "we already have a tool" — revisit with the switcher offer
- Current clients using a competitor for forms — suggest consolidation
