## Scale Package Definition

**Price:** $4,997 setup + $497/month (3-5x the Starter tier)

**Positioning:** For established businesses spending $2,000+/month on advertising who need their website to convert that traffic into customers. This isn't a website package — it's a conversion optimization system.

---

## Deliverables

### Setup Phase (Weeks 1-3)

**1. Discovery & Strategy (Week 1)**
- 90-minute strategy session (business goals, target customer, competitive landscape)
- Full competitor audit — top 5 competitors scored on speed, conversion, mobile, lead capture
- Customer journey mapping — from search to lead to customer
- Ad account review (if running Google/Facebook ads) — identify landing page gaps

**2. Website Build (Weeks 1-3)**
- Up to 10 custom-designed pages
- Mobile-first, loads under 1.5 seconds
- Homepage, services (individual page per service), about, contact, 2 additional
- Custom photography direction guide (what photos to take, angles, lighting)
- Schema markup for local SEO

**3. Conversion Infrastructure**
- LeadCatch Pro on all pages (customized per page/service)
- FAQ chatbot trained on 25+ questions (deeper than standard 15)
- Exit-intent popup with offer capture
- Click-to-call tracking integration
- Google reviews carousel on homepage and service pages

**4. Ad Landing Pages (2 included)**
- Dedicated landing pages for top 2 ad campaigns
- Headline matches ad copy exactly
- Single CTA, no navigation distractions
- A/B test variants ready (version A and B for each)

### Monthly Deliverables ($497/month)

**1. Performance Reporting**
- Monthly analytics report delivered first Monday of the month
- Key metrics: traffic, leads, conversion rate, page speed, chatbot conversations
- Personalized 5-10 minute Loom walkthrough explaining the data and recommendations

**2. Optimization**
- A/B testing on 1-2 elements per month (headlines, CTAs, form fields, button colors)
- Page speed monitoring and maintenance
- Chatbot optimization (review conversations, add/update Q&As)
- LeadCatch form performance review

**3. Content Updates**
- Up to 5 content changes per month (text updates, new photos, seasonal banners)
- Blog post or landing page creation (1 per quarter included)
- Google Business Profile post (1 per week)

**4. Support**
- Priority email support (same-day response, Mon-Fri)
- Monthly 15-minute check-in call (optional, client can skip)
- Slack channel for quick questions (response within 4 hours)

## Who This Is For

**Ideal Scale client:**
- Revenue $500K-5M/year
- Spending $2,000+/month on Google Ads or Facebook Ads
- Currently converting website traffic at 1-2% (should be 3-5%)
- Has 3+ services they offer
- Wants data-driven optimization, not just a pretty site
- Values speed and priority support

**Red flags (not a good Scale fit):**
- No existing traffic and no ad budget planned
- Needs a simple brochure site (Starter is better)
- Wants to manage everything themselves (Growth is better)
- Budget-focused (they'll churn at $497/mo)
