## The Ladder Philosophy

Every prospect enters at $0 and can climb to $997/month. Each rung proves value and creates demand for the next. Never sell the top tier to a cold prospect — let them climb.

## Key Rules

1. **Never skip rungs.** A cold prospect shouldn't see the $997/mo offer. They start with the free audit or $27 product.
2. **Let results sell the upgrade.** Show them their numbers and paint the picture of what the next tier would do.
3. **Grandfather existing clients.** When you raise prices, existing clients keep their rate. This builds loyalty and they hear about price increases from future clients, reinforcing their good deal.
4. **60% of Starter clients upgrade within 6 months.** Design the Starter experience to make Growth feel inevitable.
5. **Track average rung entry and time-to-upgrade.** This data tells you where your funnel is strongest and weakest.
