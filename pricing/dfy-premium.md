## Sales Approach

### How to identify DFY prospects:

1. **From existing clients:** Scale package clients who ask "can you just handle everything?" are DFY candidates
2. **From agency connections:** Other agencies that build brands/content but don't do web development or lead gen
3. **From cold outreach:** Businesses spending $5K+/month on ads with websites converting under 2%
4. **From inbound:** Prospects who say "I don't have time to manage this" on discovery calls

Based on your average customer value of $[X] and target of [X] new customers per month, the system needs to generate [calculated number] to pay for itself. In our experience, businesses like yours typically see [X-Y] leads per month within 60 days."

## Operational Requirements

To deliver DFY at this level, you need:

1. **Time:** Each DFY client requires ~10-15 hours/month of management
2. **Tools:** Google Ads access, Facebook Ads access, Make.com (for automations), Brevo (for email sequences), analytics access
3. **Capacity:** Max 5 DFY Standard clients or 3 DFY Agency clients simultaneously as a solo operator. Beyond that, you need to hire.
4. **Process:** Weekly check on all active ad campaigns, monthly reporting cadence, quarterly strategy reviews
