# Founder's Call Play — DM 30 Warm Contacts, Offer 15-Min Strategy Call
# 24-hour blitz to fill the pipeline
# Updated: April 2026

---

## The Play

In 24 hours, DM 30 warm contacts offering a free 15-minute strategy call about their website/lead generation. Goal: book 8-10 calls within 72 hours, close 2-3 into paying clients.

---

## Who to DM (The 30)

Build your list from these categories:

**Category 1: Past clients (5-8 people)**
People you've worked with before who might need more, or who can refer you.

**Category 2: Warm leads who never closed (5-8 people)**
People who replied to outreach, had a conversation, but never bought. They already know you.

**Category 3: LinkedIn connections in your ICP (5-8 people)**
Local business owners you've connected with, commented on their posts, or engaged with.

**Category 4: Friends/family who own businesses (3-5 people)**
Don't overthink this. If your uncle runs a plumbing company, he's on the list.

**Category 5: People who've engaged with your content (3-5 people)**
Anyone who's liked, commented on, or shared your posts recently.

---

## The DM Script (Customize per category)

### For Past Clients:

"Hey [First Name]! It's been a while — hope [Business Name] is doing great.

I'm blocking off some time this week for free 15-minute strategy calls to help business owners maximize their website leads heading into [next quarter/season].

Since we've worked together before, I thought of you first. Even if everything's running smooth, I'd love to take a quick look and share any new opportunities I see.

Want me to save you a spot? I've got [Day] at [Time] or [Day] at [Time] open."

### For Warm Leads Who Never Closed:

"Hey [First Name], Dan here. We chatted a while back about [Business Name]'s website — wanted to circle back.

I'm doing something this week: free 15-minute strategy calls where I look at your website and give you 3 specific things to improve for more leads. No pitch, no follow-up sales call — just straight advice.

I've learned a lot since we last talked and I think I could give you some really useful insights. Want a spot? I have [Day] at [Time] or [Day] at [Time]."

### For LinkedIn Connections:

"Hey [First Name], I've been enjoying your posts about [specific topic]. Love what you're doing with [Business Name].

I'm opening up a few 15-minute strategy call slots this week — free, no strings. I'll look at your website and give you 3 actionable things to increase leads.

I do this for a living and just want to connect with more local business owners. If you're interested, I've got [Day] at [Time] or [Day] at [Time]. Let me know!"

### For Friends/Family:

"Hey [First Name]! Quick question — would [Business Name] benefit from getting more customers from your website?

I'm blocking off time this week for quick 15-minute calls where I review someone's website and give them specific improvements. Free — just genuinely want to help.

If you're interested, let me know and I'll send you a time that works."

### For Content Engagers:

"Hey [First Name], noticed you've been engaging with my posts — really appreciate that!

I wanted to offer something back: I'm doing a handful of free 15-minute strategy calls this week. I'll look at your website, identify 3 things to improve, and give you a mini action plan.

No pitch at the end — just value. Want a spot?"

## The Call Structure (15 minutes, tight)

### Minutes 0-2: Rapport
"Hey [First Name], great to connect. Before we dive in, quick question — how's business going overall? Any big wins or challenges lately?"

### Minutes 2-5: Their Situation
"Tell me about your website — where does your traffic come from? How many leads are you getting per month? What's working and what's not?"

### Minutes 5-10: The Audit
"I actually looked at your site before this call. Here's what I noticed..."
[Share screen, walk through 3 specific issues. Use PageSpeed, mobile view, form analysis.]

### Minutes 10-13: Recommendations
"If I were to fix this, here's what I'd prioritize:
1. [Quick win they can do themselves]
2. [Bigger fix that requires professional help]
3. [The chatbot/LeadCatch angle if relevant]"

### Minutes 13-15: The Natural Close
"That's the overview. You could definitely tackle #1 yourself this week. For #2 and #3, those are things I do for local businesses — if you want me to put together a proposal for [Business Name], I can have it in your inbox by tomorrow. No pressure either way — glad we could connect."

## Follow-Up After the Call

### If they expressed interest (send within 2 hours):

**Subject:** Recap + proposal for [Business Name]

"Hi [First Name],

Great chatting today. As promised, here's a quick recap of what we discussed:

**3 things to fix on [Business Name]'s website:**
1. [Issue 1 + recommendation]
2. [Issue 2 + recommendation]
3. [Issue 3 + recommendation]

**My recommendation:** The [Growth/Scale] package would address all three. Here's what's included:
[List key deliverables]

**Investment:** $[X] setup + $[X]/month
**Timeline:** 10-14 days to launch
**Guarantee:** At least one qualified lead within 30 days or full refund on setup fee

Want to move forward? Just reply 'yes' and I'll send over the contract.

— Dan"

### If they seemed lukewarm (send within 24 hours):

**Subject:** 3 things to fix on [Business Name]'s website

"Hi [First Name],

Thanks for chatting today. Here's a summary of the 3 things I'd fix on your website:

1. [Issue 1 — include the quick DIY fix]
2. [Issue 2 — explain the impact]
3. [Issue 3 — what it's costing them]

#1 is something you can do this week — should take about 15 minutes.

For #2 and #3, I'm happy to help whenever the timing is right. My door is always open.

Thanks again for your time, [First Name].

— Dan"

## Timeline

**Hour 0-2:** Build the list of 30 people. Pull names from CRM, LinkedIn, phone contacts.
**Hour 2-4:** Send all 30 DMs. Personalize each one (no copy-paste without customization).
**Hour 4-24:** Monitor replies. Respond within 30 minutes. Book calls immediately.
**Day 2-3:** Conduct calls. Send follow-ups within 2 hours of each call.
**Day 4-7:** Follow up on proposals sent. Close deals.

**Expected results:**
- 30 DMs sent → 15-18 replies (50-60% from warm contacts)
- 15-18 replies → 8-10 calls booked
- 8-10 calls → 3-4 proposals sent
- 3-4 proposals → 2-3 closed deals
- Revenue: $4,000-7,500 from a 24-hour effort
